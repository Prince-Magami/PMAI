About PMAI

PMAI (Prince Magami AI) is a powerful, fast, and user-friendly web-based AI platform built to serve Nigerians of all backgrounds. Whether you're a student, business owner, or someone looking for advice, PMAI offers real-time support across different life areas.

Modes

  • Chatbox: Casual, intelligent chat interface for any topic.
  • Email/Link Scanner: Detects scam or phishing links and provides risk analysis.
  • Academic Assistant: Offers study tips, WAEC/JAMB help, and learning advice.
  • Cybersecurity Tips: Provides cyber hygiene knowledge in simple terms.

Why I Built PMAI

I built PMAI to empower everyday people with cutting-edge AI tools in a way they can understand and benefit from. The motivation came from my love for technology, helping people, and solving local problems with global innovation.

Goals

My goal is to continuously upgrade PMAI with smarter tools, local language support, and more Nigerian-specific features to ensure everyone – from tech-savvy students to small business owners – can use AI effectively.
